lightbulb The Coin Analogy

Imagine a coin on a table. In classical physics, it's either **Heads** or **Tails**. That's a regular bit.

Now, imagine **spinning** that coin. While it's spinning on its edge, it isn't definitely Heads, and it isn't definitely Tails. It's in a blur of both states. That blur is **Superposition**.

State: 0

The coin is static on Heads.

State: Superposition

The coin is spinning rapidly.
